Zarot raised an eyebrow, he couldn't believe what he was hearing. Mariel and Nirosh's eyes were wide with shock.


Elva, Liv and Tilda gasped. They had heard such sums only in movies or on the news, and it wasn't always literally money.


They didn't know exactly what their debt was, it was one of the things Fromund never talked about, but by working together they managed to calculate how much Fromund was roughly paying per month.


Elva was good at math, she together with Liv and Tilda spent a lot of time to find out what levels the Phantoms had and how much they earned per month on average, given the number of missions they took on.


Though it was a rough and not the most accurate calculation, it was the best they had.

'How much debt do we have if that money isn't enough to pay it off? Fucking hell...'

"Too much." Zarot waved his hand, snorting, he wouldn't even consider such a sum as an initial bid.


"210 million." Fromund calmly replied, as if he hadn't heard what Zarot just said.


Zarot frowned, clenching his fists tightly, he wanted to voice his displeasure, but someone beat him to it.

Nirosh's veins swelled, his face filled with anger as he clenched his fists tightly.

"Nirosh, sit back." Zarot calmly said in a heavy voice.


"But, father! He's extorting money from us! It's an impossible amount!" Nirosh was at a loss.

"60 million. That's my first bid." Zarot realized that it would take some time to arrive at a final total.

"Good, that's something to work with." Fromund shrugged, about to name his next proposal.



Elva, Liv, and Tilda continued to eavesdrop on their conversation, occasionally their eyes got wide when they heard sums, either from Fromund or Zarot. They did it quickly, but the negotiations were tough, each trying to get as much as they could.


The girls hoped Fromund could get as much money as possible.
